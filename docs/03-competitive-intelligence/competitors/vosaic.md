# Competitor Analysis: Vosaic

**Status:** Draft | **Category:** Video observation + AI feedback (K-12, higher ed, clinical)

## Executive Summary

Vosaic (FACTS/Nelnet) targets **video annotation workflows** with growing **AI analysis**—talk-time, transcription, tagging—plus strong **compliance** story for education and healthcare adjacencies.

---

## Product Surface **[FACT]**

- Browser/iOS capture, upload, duration-based tagging
- Clip comments (text/video replies), highlight reels
- Automated transcription, **talk-time analysis**
- AI-powered feedback (marketing; depth varies by module)
- LMS: Canvas, Brightspace
- Privacy: encryption, COPPA/FERPA/HIPAA claims, face blur, PII redaction, SAML SSO

---

## Inferred Stack

| Component | Likelihood |
|-----------|------------|
| Web SPA + iOS native | High |
| SaaS multi-tenant Postgres + object storage | High |
| FFmpeg-based transcode | Medium |
| Cloud ASR | High |
| CV engagement | Low–medium (blur suggests limited retention of face data) |

---

## Strengths

- **Tagging UX** for researchers and coaches (granular moment marking)
- **Compliance packaging** (FERPA, HIPAA crossover for sim)
- **LMS integrations** for higher ed
- Corporate backing (Nelnet) for enterprise sales

## Weaknesses

- AI differentiation vs **TeachFX** (audio) or **China CV** platforms not proven publicly
- Risk of being "video tool + ASR" without pedagogical ontology
- Higher ed / sim focus may dilute K-12 classroom CV investment

---

## PedagogyX Opportunities

- Deeper **pedagogical ontology** (not just tags)
- **Longitudinal analytics** across constructs
- **District norm benchmarking** with ethical aggregation
- **Multimodal fusion** beyond talk-time
