# Competitor Analysis: TeachFX

**Status:** Draft | **Category:** Audio-first classroom discourse analytics

## Executive Summary

TeachFX proves **audio-only** can scale teacher PD with **talk ratio** and discourse-pattern feedback—minimal hardware, strong research narrative.

---

## Product Surface **[FACT]**

- Mobile app records lesson audio (phone/tablet/laptop)
- Metrics: teacher talk, student talk, group talk, silence
- Patterns: academic vocabulary, open questions, praise, wait time, turn-taking
- Private teacher data; collective school growth tracking
- Published district outcome claims (e.g., +31% student talk Iron County)

---

## Inferred Pipeline

````text
Audio capture → Cloud ASR + diarization → Talk metrics + pattern classifiers → Teacher feedback UI
```text

**No video** simplifies FERPA surface and cost.

---

## Strengths

- **Lowest friction** deployment
- Clear **pedagogical thesis** (students must speak)
- Strong **GTM story** for equity (EL, Title I)
- Proven district references

## Weaknesses

- No **visual evidence** (board, slides, student nonverbals)
- Vulnerable to **noisy classrooms** and overlap speech
- Limited **admin video observation** use cases

---

## PedagogyX Positioning

**[HYPOTHESIS]** PedagogyX should include **TeachFX-equivalent audio tier** as v1 backbone, then upsell multimodal.
````
