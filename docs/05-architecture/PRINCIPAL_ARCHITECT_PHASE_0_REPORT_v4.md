# PedagogyX: Phase 0 Research & Strategy Report

## Introduction

As the Principal Research Architect and Lead Systems Engineer for PedagogyX, this document establishes the foundational questions, research directions, architectural strategies, and operational frameworks necessary to build a world-class multimodal AI classroom intelligence platform. We must refrain from immediate implementation, dedicating rigorous effort to technical research, systems analysis, and architectural design to build a robust, scalable, and impactful system.
