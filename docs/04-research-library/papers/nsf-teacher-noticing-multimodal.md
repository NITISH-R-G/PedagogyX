# P-005: Teacher Noticing and Student Learning in Human-AI Classrooms

|-------|-------|

| **Tags** | MM, TE |

## Problem

How do teachers allocate attention when AI tutors are present?

## Method

Multimodal signals: gaze, analytics engagement, student visits correlated with learning gains.

## Findings (high level) **[FACT]**

Teacher **deep analysis of learning analytics** showed strongest association with student learning gains in AI-supported classrooms.

## Limitations

- Specific to AI-tutor hybrid settings
- Gaze hardware not scalable everywhere

## PedagogyX Takeaway

**Analytics engagement** of the teacher may matter as much as student metrics—product should coach teachers on using evidence, not only score students.
